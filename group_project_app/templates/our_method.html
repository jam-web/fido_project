Our Method

Borrowing from the world of sports and dance, we believe that the "math muscle," is a skill. In other words, as students get better and better at math they can learn to master math.

The practical skills developed from math mastery include

  • construct logical arguments and expose illogical arguments
  • analytical thinking
  • quantitative reasoning
  • problem solving
  • communication
  • time-management

We also believe that any student can become proficient in the four arithmetic operations. Extra repetition may be needed for some students We have differing pathways for students on the traditional, accelerated, or additional support.
